Meet Gecko

Gecko is a cool little lizard with a chill vibe and a curious nature. He loves to hang out on his favorite rock, soak up the heat, and watch the world go by. He’s quiet, calm, and super low-key. Gecko enjoys climbing around his tank, hiding in cozy spots, and coming out when it’s time to eat—especially if there are crickets involved! He’s easy to take care of and doesn’t mind being gently held once he feels comfortable. Gecko would do great with someone who’s patient and likes relaxed pets. If you’re looking for a cool, quiet reptile buddy, Gecko might be the perfect match!
