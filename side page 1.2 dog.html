Meet Lima

Lima is a sweet and playful Golden Retriever with a heart of gold (and the fluff to match!). She’s always smiling, always wagging, and always ready to be your best friend. She loves long walks, playing fetch, and splashing in any puddle she can find. Lima is super friendly and loves being around people—she’ll happily follow you from room to room just to keep you company. She’s gentle, smart, and loves to learn, especially when treats or praise are involved. Cuddles on the couch? Yes, please. If you’re looking for a loving, loyal, and fun pup to brighten your days, Lima is the golden girl for you!
