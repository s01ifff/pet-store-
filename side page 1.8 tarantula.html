Meet Itsy Bitsy

Itsy Bitsy is a fuzzy little tarantula with a calm attitude and eight very cool legs. Don’t let the spooky look fool you—she’s quiet, low-maintenance, and actually pretty shy. She spends most of her time chilling in her hide, slowly exploring her tank, or just sitting still and watching the world. Itsy Bitsy isn’t the cuddly type, but she’s super fun to observe and easy to care for. She likes a cozy, quiet home with the right setup and someone who enjoys watching cool, unique pets do their thing. If you’re into awesome creatures that are more chill than creepy, Itsy Bitsy might be your perfect match!
